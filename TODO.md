## Known Bugs / Issues ⚠️

- [ ] ⚠️ Google Drive folder import only works for single files (not full folder) — waiting for OAuth
- [ ] ⚠️ html2canvas may not capture cross-origin images from external URLs — affected pages fall back to blank
- [ ] ⚠️ Very large albums (40+ pages) may be slow to generate PDF in browser — consider server-side rendering
- [ ] ⚠️ Thumbnail sidebar in PreviewStep uses CSS transform scaling — may be blurry on retina displays
- [ ] ⚠️ HEIC images from iPhone not natively supported in all browsers — need conversion library
- [ ] ⚠️ Processing step re-runs on hot reload in dev — add persistent flag to sessionStorage
